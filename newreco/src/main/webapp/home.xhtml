Bring Process Intelligence To Your Secured Debt Recovery

Nationwide Recovery Services brings intelligence to the process of recovering secured debt. Lenders and their third-party service providers now can realize the efficiencies of applying their specific business rules to the recovery process.

NRS' solution provides financial institutions new capabilities to manage all of the professionals required to turn defaulted collateral into revenue generating assets. Enabling effective communication between lenders and their legal, real estate, property management and appraisal professionals combined with industry-leading workflow management ensures the most effective use of resources to complete the recovery cycle.

From the initial population of all loan particulars from the Lender's servicing platform the NRS system brings new intelligence to: Lender-specific workflow processes Secure communications between business partners Management of service provider performance Performance reporting
